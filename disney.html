Welcome to the Magical World of Disney!

Disney ek aisi duniya hai jo har kisi ke bachpan ki yaadon se judi hoti hai. Yeh sirf ek company nahi, balki ek sapno ka sansar hai jisme kahaniyan jeevit hoti hain. Walt Disney ne 1923 me iski shuruaat ki thi aur tab se lekar aaj tak, Disney entertainment aur creativity ka doosra naam ban chuka hai. Disneyland aur Disney movies har umar ke logon ko ek magical experience dete hain. Chaahe Mickey Mouse ki masti ho ya Frozen ki kahani, har pal ek naye sapne ka ehsaas dilata hai. Yahaan har kahani ek nayi duniya ka darwaza kholti hai, jisme sirf khushiyan aur adventure hote hain. Disney ka motto hai – "The Happiest Place on Earth," jo har visitor aur dekhne wale ke chehre par muskurahat le aata hai!
